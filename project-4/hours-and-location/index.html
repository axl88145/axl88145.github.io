About Us

Laetus Café is a humble and comfortable, creative space with home-made baked goods and coffee.

With a second-hand styled interior, Customers have the option of relaxing with their drinks and snacks, and also a warm space to paint and create too.

We also host fun group outing events, called Paint Nights, for friends, families, and colleagues can enjoy.

This warm, welcoming environment will inspire people to create when seeing others creating.
