Don Quixote

Don Quixote is a novel about a middle-aged man in La Mancha, Spain, who is addicted to stories of chivalry. One day, he no longer can tell fact from fiction and decides he is now a knight-errant. He puts on an old suit of armor, takes his old horse out to ride, and chooses a random peasant woman as his lady love to fight for.

Quixote now sets out on an adventure, and recruits a loyal sidekick, Sancho Panza, to help battle evil, save those in distress, and have the time of their lives!
